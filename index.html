About Sofia

Yoga for every
body & every soul

I'm Sofia — a Seattle-based yoga instructor with a passion for creating space where movement meets mindfulness. My classes blend dynamic flow, breath awareness, and moments of stillness to help you feel truly at home in your body.

Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or deepening an established practice, I believe yoga is for everyone. My signature Glow Flow method draws from Vinyasa, Yin, and somatic principles to create a practice that is both challenging and deeply nourishing.
