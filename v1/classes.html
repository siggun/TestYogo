The Offerings

Find Your Practice

We don't chase a body type — we chase awareness and presence, on and off the mat. Whatever path brings you here, there's space for you.

01

Studio Classes

Drop into any of our regularly scheduled group classes. We move through breath, flow, and stillness together — no experience required, all bodies welcome. Classes vary in style and intensity so there's always something that meets you where you are.

Specialized Workshops

Monthly deep-dives into specific themes — breathwork & pranayama, somatic awareness, yin & restoration, seasonal rituals, and more. Workshops are immersive, intentional, and limited to small groups so every participant gets real attention.

2–3 Hours Small Group Monthly

03

Private Lessons

Completely tailored to you. We start with a conversation about where you are and where you want to go, then build a practice around that. Private sessions accelerate growth in a way group classes can't — you move at your own pace with undivided attention.

60–90 min 1-on-1 In-Studio or Virtual

Private Group Sessions

Bring your people. Perfect for bachelorette parties, friend groups, corporate wellness days, birthday celebrations, or any occasion that deserves something meaningful. We'll create a custom experience just for your group of 2–8 people.
