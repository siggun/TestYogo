Common Questions

FAQs

Do I need experience to take a class?

Not at all. Every class is designed to welcome all levels. I offer modifications for everything so you're always working within your own range — never pushing past what feels right.

What should I bring to class?

Comfortable, breathable clothes and water. Mats are available to rent for $3, or bring your own. Props — blocks, straps, blankets — are provided.

How do I book a private session?

Send a message through the form above or email directly. I'll follow up to schedule a quick discovery call so we can figure out exactly what you need before your first session.

What's your cancellation policy?

Studio classes: cancel up to 4 hours before with no charge. Private sessions: 24-hour cancellation notice required. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes — private 1-on-1 sessions are available virtually via Zoom. You get the same personalized attention from wherever you are.

What makes your classes different?

We move in ways that liberate, nourish, and empower. We're not chasing a body type — we chase awareness and presence on and off the mat. Movement here is medicine, and connection is the goal.
