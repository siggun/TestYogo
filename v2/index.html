About Your Instructor

I teach yoga for people who are ready to feel at home in their own body. My classes aren't about hitting shapes or chasing flexibility milestones. They're about learning to listen, to breathe, to trust what you feel.

Whether you're stepping on the mat for the very first time or deepening a practice you've held for years — there's space here for you. Not louder. Not harder. Deeper.

We bridge the gap between mind, body, and awareness so that movement becomes the medicine it's always been.
